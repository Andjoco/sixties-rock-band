The Happening
'vintage sounds, modern mood'
THE HAPPENING, are a four-piece covers band, who met through a mutual love of 1960's music. We formed the band because of the fun we have and the energy we create, by playing these amazing songs to an audience. The 1960's is a little before our time, but we think this gives us an edge. The songs we play are definitely from that era, but we add a unique freshness to them.

So if you're looking for something a little different for your wedding, party or any social gathering, we could be just what you're looking for. We're also happy to discuss what songs you'd like us to perform and can change our set list to your preference.

Please scroll down the page, to hear and see us for yourselves. Then if you'd like to know more, contact us to provide some details about you and your event and we will schedule a time to talk to you.
